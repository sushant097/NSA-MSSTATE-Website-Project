Embark on Your Journey to MSU

Here are the key airport options and travel tips:

  • ✈️ GTR (Golden Triangle Regional Airport): 20 mins from Starkville. University shuttle and Nepali friends might assist with pickup.
  • ✈️ Jackson-Medgar Wiley Evers International Airport: ~2 hrs drive.
  • ✈️ Memphis International Airport: ~2 hr 41 mins drive.
  • ✈️ Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport: ~4 hr 30 mins drive.
  • ✈️ Birmingham-Shuttlesworth International Airport: ~2 hr 23 mins drive.
Tip: GTR may be slightly more expensive but is the most convenient. For other airports, plan Uber rides or check in with the NSA Messenger group for possible pickups.
Important: Confirm your ride before landing. Avoid distant airports unless transportation is arranged.
